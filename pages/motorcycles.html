Harley Davidson Sportster Nightster 2022

At the heart of the 2022 Sportster Nightster is the new Revolution Max 975T engine. It's a liquid-cooled 60-degree V-Twin with a torque curve that stays flat across the powerband, and performance designed to deliver strong acceleration and solid mid-range power. The Harley Davidson Sportster Nightster has traction control and engine brake control, as well as three driving modes.
